In psychology and medicine a syndrome is when there are multiple symptom s These symptoms may be related In some cases the way in which they are related is known in other cases it is not When both the relationship of symptoms and their causes are known people generally talk about a disease Symptoms that occur together in similar contexts are often related or have a common cause Some syndromes such as Down syndrome have only one cause In the case of Down syndrome the cause is known Other syndromes such as Pakinsonian syndrome may have many causes and finding the exact cause may be difficult
