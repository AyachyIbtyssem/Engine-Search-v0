 Trichotillomania is an impulse control disorder It causes a person to habitually pull out their hair People with trichotillomania may pull hair from anywhere on the body Sometimes people with trich end up having large areas on the body without hair Trichotillomania is listed in the DSM IV Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders The DSM is written by the American psychiatry Psychiatric Association It lists every condition which is officially called a mental illness The DSM puts trich in the impulse control disorder category However there are questions about what category trich should be in At different times it may seem to be a habit an addiction a tic disorder or an obsessive compulsive disorder
